# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Vietnamese high school students preparing for the Digital SAT exam. Their context is focused study sessions where concentration is critical. The job to be done is practicing full-length tests, reviewing mistakes, and building core vocabulary.

## Product Purpose
Deliver a structured, distraction-free testing environment modeled exactly after the official College Board Bluebook app. It exists to help students practice under authentic test conditions while sustaining motivation through a gamified points system and progress tracking.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, focused, structured. 
- **Voice:** Academic but encouraging, clear, and direct.
- **Emotional goals:** Induce focus, reduce cognitive load, build confidence through authentic simulation.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, noisy UIs with sidebars, footers, or ads during testing.
- Overly playful or cartoonish aesthetics that detract from the seriousness of a standardized test.
- The 2014-era app look; shadow-heavy or glassmorphism-heavy UI.

## Design Principles
1. **Focus Above All:** The testing interface must eliminate all chrome and navigation to simulate real exam conditions.
2. **Authentic Simulation:** Match the split-screen paradigm and workflow of the official Digital SAT app.
3. **Transparent Progression:** Ensure students can easily see their history and understand where they went wrong.
4. **Gamified but Grounded:** Use points to motivate, but keep the visual execution restrained and academic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- Minimum tap targets 44×44px.
- Keyboard-navigable test interface for efficient review and accessibility.
