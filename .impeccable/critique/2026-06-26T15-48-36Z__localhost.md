#### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: This looks heavily AI-generated. The neon purple primary button with a large glowing drop shadow, combined with low-opacity scattered icons (brain, book, graduation cap) in the background, is a classic AI interpretation of "modern dark mode education". It feels more like a generic template than a grounded, academic SAT prep platform. 

**Deterministic scan**: The detector found 2 issues:
- `gray-on-color` (Warning): Gray text on colored background found in `ExamManagerPanel.tsx`. 
- `overused-font` (Warning): Space Grotesk used in `index.css`. The AI-generated feel is amplified by this font choice.

#### Overall Impression
The layout is standard and functional, but the aesthetic is distracted by glowing effects and unnecessary background noise that contradicts the "distraction-free" product principle. The login CTA is also confusingly labeled.

#### What's Working
- **High Contrast**: The main white text ("SAT", "Chào mừng trở lại") reads very well against the dark background.
- **Clear Structure**: The split layout (branding on the left, login on the right) is a proven, reliable pattern.

#### Priority Issues
- **[P1] Confusing primary CTA**: There is an email input field, but the primary button below it says "Tiếp tục với tài khoản Demo" (Continue with Demo account). If a user enters their own email, this button label creates immediate hesitation. 
  - **Why it matters**: Breaks the core login flow and causes cognitive friction.
  - **Fix**: Rename to "Tiếp tục" (Continue) or "Đăng nhập" (Log in). Move the Demo account option to a separate secondary button if it's a distinct flow.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable clarify`
- **[P2] Neon glow and scattered icons**: The background features floating outline icons, and the primary button has a heavy glowing shadow.
  - **Why it matters**: Contradicts the "strict, neutral" Design Principles outlined in `PRODUCT.md`. It feels game-like in a cheap way rather than "gamified but grounded".
  - **Fix**: Remove the background icons. Flatten the button shadow to a crisp, subtle elevation rather than a neon glow.
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable quieter`
- **[P2] Overused Typography**: Using Space Grotesk.
  - **Why it matters**: Contributes to the generic AI-template feel.
  - **Fix**: Switch to a more grounded, academic display face (e.g., a modern serif or a sturdier sans).
  - **Suggested command**: `/impeccable typeset`

#### Persona Red Flags

**Jordan (First-Timer)**: Will be confused by the main CTA. "I just typed my email, but the button says 'Continue with Demo account'. Will it use my email or a fake one?"

**Sam (Accessibility)**: The input field's border is very subtle against the dark background. While the focus ring helps, the resting state is hard to distinguish as an input field for low-vision users.
